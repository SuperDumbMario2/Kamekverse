{{name}} Manners

The following are some important guidelines for making {{name}} a fun and enjoyable experience for everyone.

The {{name}} Code of Conduct contains detailed information, so please read it carefully.

Posts Can Be Viewed Around the World

{{name}} contains many communities where people from all over the world can share their thoughts.

When you post in a community, remember that everyone can see it. Use common sense, and think before you post. {{name}} is a service that is also accessible from the Internet, so bear in mind that people who don't use {{name}} may also see your posts.

Additionally, any comments you make on your friends’ posts will be seen not by just your friends but by people around the world, too. Please keep this in mind.

That doesn't mean you can complain about someone calling out a minority or saying a slur beacuse "it's offensive". Hard hate speech like threats or harrasment is not allowed on Kamekverse but remember that this site is NOT left-leaning

Also, this rule isn't enforced as much in private communities.

Do Not Post Personal Information—Yours or Others’

Remember, knowing someone in {{name}} isn’t the same as knowing them in real life.

Never share your real name, home address, work or school name, or other personally identifying information with anyone on {{name}}, and never share anyone else's information either.

Additionally, if someone you meet in {{name}} invites you to meet him or her in the real world, do not accept. {{name}} is an online community and should not be used to arrange real-world meet-ups.

Don’t Post Spoilers (on game communities)

Some people come to {{name}} looking for tips and tricks for games, but others want to discover a game's secrets all on their own.

Posts that reveal secrets of a game or its story are called "spoilers."

If you're posting something about a game that might be a spoiler, be sure to check the Spoilers box before sending your post.

This way, people who don't want to be spoiled won't see your post.

Users Aged 12 and Under

Due to COPPA, we cannot allow people under 13 on Kamekverse. We usually don't use "maturity" signs to assume someone's age, so in most of cases to get banned for being underage there should be at least one direct sign of you being underage.

Code of Conduct Violations

Our goal is to keep {{name}} fun and enjoyable for everyone.

Violation Types

Additionally, the following content is prohibited from being posted or included in messages by the {{name}} Code of Conduct.

  • Personal Information

    Personal information includes but is not limited to your IRL name, home address, work or school name, and phone number, and in some cases your e-mail address. Never use {{name}} as a means of setting up real-world meet-ups. Never write or ask in public for account information or IDs for other services, or any information that would allow people to be contacted directly.

  • Violent Content

    This kind of content includes anything that depicts violence, promotes suicide, or endorses acts of cruelty or violence.

  • Inappropriate/Harmful

    Inappropriate or harmful content includes anything that promotes dangerous behavior or illegal activities.

  • Hateful/Bullying

    This includes any content that slanders, defames, or in another way harasses another person or a group of people.

  • Sexually Explicit

    Sexually explicit content includes anything containing nudity, sexuality, or propositions.

  • Inappropriate Reporting

    Intentionally misreporting a post for violating the {{name}} Code of Conduct is itself a violation of the {{name}} Code of Conduct.

  • Other

    Additional kinds of violation include intentionally posting the following:

    • Content that infringes on the copyrights, intellectual-property rights, usage of likeness, or privacy rights of any third party
    • Content that disrupts the community (spam, empty posts, etc.)
    • Content related to the borrowing, lending, or transferal of goods or money.
