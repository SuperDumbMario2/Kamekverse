About Links

Linking to pages on Miiverse is generally acceptable so long as the link encompasses an entire post and does not link only to a section of a post.

How do I use Miiverse?

To use Miiverse, you need either a Wii U console or a system in the Nintendo 3DS family. Whichever of these devices you use must be connected to the Internet.
Once you have created a Nintendo Network ID using either a Wii U console or a system in the Nintendo 3DS family, you can launch Miiverse from that device.

Your ID will also allow access to Miiverse from PCs, smartphones, and other non-Nintendo devices.
